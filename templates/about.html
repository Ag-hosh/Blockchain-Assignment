About Us

Charity for All is a nonprofit organization dedicated to eradicating childhood hunger and providing education and support to underprivileged children in India. Our mission is to make a positive impact on the lives of young ones, offering them hope, sustenance, and education.

We believe that every Indian child deserves a chance to thrive and fulfill their potential. Through the generous support of individuals like you, we can make a difference in the lives of these children, providing them with the tools they need for a brighter future.

Join us in our mission to end childhood hunger and provide education to those in need. Together, we can create a better future for the children of India.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eradicate childhood hunger and provide quality education to underprivileged children in India. We are committed to making a lasting impact on the lives of these young ones, offering them the chance to thrive and build a brighter future.
